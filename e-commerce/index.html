The world of e-commerce is large and ever-growing and ever-evolving. But the changes in e-commerce over the past year (or year-and-a-half) alone were remarkable. Globally, retail e-commerce sales in 2020 neared $4.3 trillion compared to $3.35 trillion in 2019, according to Statista. In 2021, this figure rose 16.8% to $4.92 billion.
